Web Site Topic

SCUBA dive club web presence. Site name is Seahorse Dive Club.

Target Audience

Boeing employees that are SCUBA divers. Most of these people are engineers, and the majority appear to be men between the ages of 40-60. THere are also a few younger members. All would be over 22.

Purpose of the Site

The purpose of the site is to provide information about the club meetings, membership, link to facebook posts, information about the club, photo gallery, and library materials.

Comperable Sites

  • Emerald Sea Dive Club
  • New England Aquarium Dive Club, Inc.
  • The Dive Club

Personality

The website should express the wonder and beauty of the world under the sea.

Limiting Factors

The users will access the website on a variety of devices, including mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.
